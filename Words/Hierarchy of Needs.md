Often known as the Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs, because it was formalized by Abraham Maslow. This is a layout of priorities that must be solved in someone's life as a requirement for happiness. Gaps in someone's life of these needs and even swapping priority in one's life often leads to dysfunctional behavior, [[Depression and Anxiety]].
